A System That Fits Into Your Coaching

The platform works alongside regular training without changing the coaching process. Coaches continue their sessions as usual, and with a simple tap, important moments are recorded through audio or video. Each session automatically generates daily reports and contributes to weekly progress summaries, creating a clear record of training activity.

Identify Areas for Improvement

When training information is consistently recorded and reviewed, areas that need improvement become easier to recognize. Patterns across sessions reveal technical weaknesses and skills that require additional focus. This structured view of training helps ensure that adjustments are based on clear observations rather than assumptions.
