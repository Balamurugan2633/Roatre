# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Roatre (Root Athlete Rise) is a sports technology platform designed to empower athletes and coaches through data-driven insights and comprehensive management tools. The platform serves as a bridge between athletic talent and coaching expertise, providing personalized support for performance optimization and team management.

## Value Proposition
- **For Athletes**: Unlock full potential through smart insights, advanced performance tracking, tailored nutrition guidance, and exposure tools that help build standout profiles for coaches and scouts
- **For Coaches**: All-in-one coaching assistant that handles team management, talent scouting, and player monitoring with data-powered tools, reducing administrative burden while enhancing coaching effectiveness

## Key Features

### Athlete Services
- Advanced performance tracking and analytics
- Personalized nutrition and training support
- Profile building for coach and scout visibility
- Smart insights for faster growth and smarter training
- Exposure tools to showcase athletic achievements

### Coach Services
- Comprehensive team management system
- Talent scouting and player discovery tools
- Data-driven player monitoring and analytics
- Administrative automation to focus on coaching
- Scalable from local squads to elite academies

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Athletes seeking performance optimization and career advancement
- **Secondary**: Coaches and team managers at all levels (local to elite)
- **Tertiary**: Scouts and talent evaluators looking for athletic prospects

## Use Cases
- Individual athlete performance tracking and improvement
- Team roster management and player development
- Talent identification and recruitment
- Training program optimization
- Nutrition and wellness monitoring
- Athletic profile showcasing for recruitment opportunities
