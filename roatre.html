WHY ROATRE

Roatre is a sports development platform that helps athletes and coaches improve performance through structured video-based training and feedback. Athletes can record and upload their practice sessions, receive expert coaching insights, and track progress over time, while coaches can analyse technique, monitor development, and guide athletes remotely. By connecting athletes, coaches, academies and parents in one secure platform, Roatre makes sports training more measurable, accessible and performance-driven.
